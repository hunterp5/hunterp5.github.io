My Bio

A Kansas native born in the Wichita area, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry/Biochemistry from Wichita State University in 2019. During university, I performed multiple years of Organic Chemistry research and oversaw a General Chemistry laboratory course of 25 students, learning and teaching simultaneously. After graduation, I went on to work as a Research Scientist.

I have a deep appreciation for the natural sciences and it has been an incredible experience looking deeper into my surroundings to gain insights about what makes the world work. I try to capture these special moments through a lens or on paper, and post them for others to hopefully enjoy and appreciate.

The Goal of this Website:

When I made the commitment to learning Computer Science, I sought to gain a marketable skill that allows me to express myself creatively, and offer a service to those who could benefit from it. I have always been fascinated by the limitless possibilities that technology provides, and Web Development offered an excellent introduction to the world of coding, programming, and design. My long-term goal is to gain the necessary skills and programming-mindset to build applications from the ground up, while integrating my love for photography, drawing, and science.
