Bio

My name is Hunter Picard; a Kansas native born in the Wichita area, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry/Biochemistry from Wichita State University in 2019. During university, I performed multiple years of Organic Chemistry research and oversaw a General Chemistry laboratory course of 25 students, learning and teaching simultaneously. I currently work as a Chemist for a mid-size startup, specializing in research, development, and synthesis of small-molecule pharmaceuticals.

I have a deep appreciation for the natural sciences and it has been an incredible experience looking deeper into my surroundings to gain insights about what makes the world work. I try to capture these special moments through a lens or on paper, and post them for others to hopefully enjoy and appreciate. Keep up-to-date with my website to follow my journey!

The Goal of this Website:

When I made the commitment to learning how to build websites, I sought to gain a marketable skill that allows me to express myself creatively and offer a service to individuals and businesses seeking to market their services to a broader audience. I have always been fascinated by the limitless possibilities that technology provides, and Web Development offered an excellent introduction to the world of coding, programming, and design. I aspire to gain the necessary skills and programming-mindset to build both static websites and web applications specific to a customer's needs.
